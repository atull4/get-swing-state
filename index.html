About Us

I am a board game enthusiast who grew up with regular game nights playing Risk, Ticket to Ride, Sushi Go, Scythe, Skull, and more with my friends. I took that passion and paired it with copywriting skills, creativity, interest in politics, and more into this game.

Your support will help me fund the remaining work, cover for funds already put into the creation, and pay for manufacturing & shipping of the game.
